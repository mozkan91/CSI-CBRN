MELORIX

  • Atropine Sulphate
  • Pralidoxime Chloride
  • Midazolam

First Aid Against Organophosphate and Nerve Agent Exposure - Autoinjector

MELORIX is an advanced three‑component, dual‑chamber autoinjector integrating multiple active pharmaceutical ingredients for rapid emergency treatment of organophosphate and nerve agent intoxication. It delivers comprehensive antidotal therapy in a single deployment to maximize survival outcomes in chemical exposure incidents.

As a core part of the ChemProtect autoinjector portfolio, MELORIX is engineered for fast pre‑hospital intervention, enabling both Self‑Aid and Buddy‑Aid applications where immediate medical care cannot be ensured.

  • Three active pharmaceutical ingredients for comprehensive antidotal coverage
  • Dual‑chamber autoinjector supporting integrated multi‑drug delivery
  • Fast and controlled intramuscular administration
  • Ergonomic and robust construction suitable for military and tactical use
  • Reliable operation under stress, protective gear, and extreme environmental conditions

Operational Concept

  • Designed for Individual First Aid (IFA) use in CBRN scenarios
  • Suitable for Self‑Aid and Buddy‑Aid administration
  • Optimized for tactical, field, and military operational environments
  • Supports rapid delivery of multiple antidotes in a single device

Intended Use

  • Acute organophosphate poisoning
  • Nerve agent exposure (G‑series and V‑series agents)
  • CBRN incidents and chemical warfare scenarios
  • Emergency pre‑hospital antidote administration

User Group / Scope of Use

  • Armed Forces
  • CBRN Defense Units
  • Law Enforcement Agencies
  • Special Operations Forces and Tactical Medical Personnel

    • *Not intended for civilian use

Packaging & Logistics

  • Single-use single-chamber semi-automatic autoinjector
  • Tactical-ready configuration for field deployment
  • Packaging, shelf life, and logistics details available upon request

Contents

    Active Ingredients:
  • Atropine Sulfate: 2mg / 2ml
  • Pralidoxime Chloride: 500mg / 2ml
  • Midazolam: 3.3mg / 1ml
