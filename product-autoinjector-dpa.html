DOUBLEPEN PA 2.0

  • Pralidoxime Chloride
  • Atropine Sulphate

First Aid Against Organophosphate and Nerve Agent Exposure - Autoinjector

DOUBLEPEN PA is a dual-chamber semi-automatic autoinjector containing Pralidoxime Chloride and Atropine Sulfate, developed for emergency first aid treatment of organophosphate and nerve agent poisoning.

The device is designed for rapid antidote administration during the critical pre-hospital phase following chemical exposure. DOUBLEPEN PA supports immediate medical response in CBRN incidents, particularly under military and tactical operational conditions.

  • Combined antidotal therapy: Pralidoxime + Atropine
  • Dual-chamber, semi-automatic autoinjector system
  • Rapid and controlled intramuscular administration
  • Ergonomic and robust design suitable for military use
  • Reliable operation under stress, protective equipment, and harsh environmental conditions

Operational Concept

  • Designed according to Individual First Aid (IFA) principles
  • Suitable for Self‑Aid and Buddy‑Aid applications
  • Optimized for chemical threat environments and tactical field operations
  • Compatible with ChemProtect dual-chamber autoinjector concept

Intended Use

  • Organophosphate poisoning
  • Nerve agent exposure (G‑series and V‑series agents)
  • CBRN incidents and chemical warfare scenarios
  • Pre-hospital emergency antidote administration

User Group / Scope of Use

  • Armed Forces
  • CBRN Defense Units
  • Law Enforcement Agencies
  • Special Operations Forces and Tactical Medical Personnel

    • *Not intended for civilian use
