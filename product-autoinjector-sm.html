SINGLEPEN M 2.0

  • Morphine Sulphate

Morphine - Autoinjector

SINGLEPEN M is a single-use auto-injector system designed to support the rapid and controlled management of severe post-traumatic pain under field and battlefield conditions. It contributes to casualty stabilization and supports safe evacuation during the pre-hospital phase of first aid.

  • Single-use SinglePen auto-injector design containing morphine sulfate
  • Automatic injection mechanism supporting rapid and controlled administration
  • Ergonomic design suitable for use under stress, with gloves, and in low-visibility conditions
  • High mechanical reliability and operational continuity
  • Durable structure resistant to harsh environmental and battlefield conditions
  • Design compliant with color coding and marking standardization for military use

Operational Concept

  • Designed in accordance with the Individual First Aid concept
  • Suitable for Self-Aid and Buddy-Aid applications
  • Configured for high-threat military and tactical operations

Intended Use

  • Management of acute and severe pain in combat and tactical operational environments
  • Field and battlefield first aid applications
  • Supportive pre-hospital intervention during casualty evacuation

User Group / Scope of Use

  • Armed Forces
  • Law Enforcement Units
  • Authorized Emergency Response and Tactical Medical Personnel

    • *Not intended for civilian use

Packaging & Logistics

  • Single-use single-chamber semi-automatic autoinjector
  • Tactical-ready configuration for field deployment
  • Packaging, shelf life, and logistics details available upon request

Contents

  • Active Ingredient: Morphine Sulfate
  • Dosage: 20mg / 2ml
