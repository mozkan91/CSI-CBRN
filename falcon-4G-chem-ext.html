Active stand-off detection uses its own energy source — a tuneable laser. SEC's patented technology employs a pulsed CO₂ laser based on the principles of Differential Absorption LIDAR (DIAL). Falcon 4G does not analyse the full absorption spectrum; instead, it evaluates absorption at two gas-specific wavelengths: λON — strongly absorbed by the gas, and λOFF — minimally absorbed by the gas.

The DIAL system has the following advantages

  • No physical contact with the agent
  • Detection of average concentration of the chemical on the detection path (not only locally)
  • Large detection range (CWA - up to 6km)
