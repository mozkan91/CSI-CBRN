RSDL - Reactive Skin Decontamination Lotion

  • KBDO (Potassium 2,3-butanedione monoximate)
  • DAM (2,3-butanedione monoxime)

RSDL® is the only FDA-cleared, CE Class IIa marked medical device designed to remove or neutralise chemical warfare agents and T-2 toxin from the skin. Additionally, it is the only CE Class IIa marked device effective against organophosphate chemicals.

The foam sponge achieves decontamination by a combination of physical removal from the skin and chemical neutralization of each chemical agent through interaction of the KBDO with the CWA within the pores of the sponge.

  • Dual MOA - Removes or neutralizes CWAs from the skin
  • Rapid neutralization within 2 minutes
  • Compact and portable
  • Single use, tear-open packet with pre-soaked sponge
  • 5 year shelf life from the date of manufacture
  • The RSDL® packaging has been tested in accordance with U.S. military standard MIL-STD-810, ensuring proven resistance to harsh operational environments

Many chemical agents penetrate human skin within seconds to minutes, initiating rapid systemic absorption. Decontamination is a medical countermeasure aimed at reducing the internal uptake of harmful substances following external exposure.

  • ~35 Years of operational experience in active war zones and emergency situations
  • +30 Countries with RSDL® included in emergency stockpiles
  • +20 Armed forces using RSDL® around the world

Trusted around the world

  • U.S. Department of Defense
  • NATO Countries
  • Emergency responders worldwide

Indication

  • Use for decontaminating skin exposed to chemical agents in emergency situations
  • The lotion neutralizes and removes CWAs, preventing further absorption and contamination

The RSDL kit is intended to remove or neutralize the following chemical warfare agents (CWAs) from the skin:
  • V-Series Nerve Agents (VX, VR)
  • G-Series Nerve Agents [Tabun (GA), Soman (GD)
  • Sarin (GB), Cyclohexyl Sarin (GF)
  • Mustard Gas (HD)
  • T-2 Toxin
    • And organophosphate pesticides (malathion, parathion, paraoxon, paraoxon-methyl and malaoxon).

  • Deployed by over 20 militaries worldwide since 2002
  • Portable design for quick response
  • Starts working within 2 minutes
  • The volume of RSDL lotion within each 21 ml packet is enough to decontaminate the average equivalent of the hands, neck, face, and inside surface of a respirator, if you are wearing one
    • * Based upon liquid phase and in vitro reactivity tests (clinical relevance unknown)

For External Use Only By Trained Individuals
During emergency situations, RSDL® does not require immediate removal from the skin, but should be rinsed as soon as it is safe to do so.
Tested in over 300+ people for effectiveness, proven safe and specifically designed for human skin.

  • Lotion for Skin Decontamination
  • Single use, tear-open packet with pre-soaked sponge
  • Compact and portable, fits any kit
  • Dosage: 21 ml and 42 ml
  • Green Packet - Active packet
  • Blue Packet - For training purposes only (No active ingredients)
  • Shelf life: 5 years
  • 21 ml: 16 cm (6.29’’) ± 0.2 cm (0.07’’) (height), 11 cm (4.33’’) ± 0.2 cm (0.07’’) (width)
  • 42 ml: 16 cm (6.29’’) ± 0.2 cm (0.07’’) (height), 16 cm (6.29’’) ± 0.2 cm (0.07’’) (width)
  • Stable Storage: Between 59°F and 86°F (15°C – 30°C)

Store in original container protected from direct sunlight in a dry, cool, and well-ventilated area, away from incompatible materials and food and drink. Keep closed and sealed until ready for use. Do not use this product if the packet is compromised.

CAUTION: Use only as directed. Not for use prior to exposure (i.e., prophylactic use) or whole body decontamination. Allow RSDL® to remain on the skin for at least two minutes. An RSDL® ingredient is absorbed through the skin and may cause adverse effects. Avoid leaving on the skin for long periods. Avoid contact with the eyes and mucous membranes. Remove RSDL® with water when conditions permit. Keep out of reach of children.
