GAMMA DETECTORS

COMO Contamination Monitor

Radiation meter for food and many more

A significant part of the radioactivity intrudes into the human body through ingestion of food. GIHMM's COMO Contamination Monitor measures and quantifies radioactivity and identifies radioactive nuclides (radioisotopes) in food and other materials.

The ease of use of COMO and its accuracy make it the ideal analysis tool for a variety of applications to identify radioactive contamination in a very short time in food, milk, water, wastewater, materials, components and many more.

    Thanks to the supplied software, the COMO Contamination Monitor identifies the radioactive nuclides I-131, Ru-103, Cs-134, Cs-137 and K-40 efficiently and precisely. The following substances and materials can be tested for possible radioactive contamination:
  • Food, milk, dairy products and juices
  • Animal Feed
  • Waste, wastewater, sewage sludge
  • Raw materials, soil (farmland)
  • Finished products, components
  • Test specimens

Easy to operate

The special feature of the COMO is the simple operation. The supplied software guides the operator step by step through the analysis process. If you exceed self-definable thresholds, you will be notified graphically and will receive a full protocol on request.

Easy Handling

  • USB connection including power supply
  • Can be used with a notebook
  • Compatible with our GAMMO TOOLs
  • Portable, installation-free software
  • Configurable measurement parameters
  • Detailed PDF protocol functions with spectrum
  • Data export in CSV format

Accurate measurement results in a very short time

Due to the large measurement chamber volume and the 35 mm thick lead shielding, COMO enables clearly better accurate measurement results, compared to similar products in this class, at a very attractive price. And the analysis process takes no longer than 10 minutes.

Extended application possibilities through Gihmm product combinations

The choice of the gamma spectrum probe GSP02 with either 2"or 3" NaI(Tl) scintillator in combination with our GAMMA TOOLs expands the application possibilities enormously:
  • Use the GSP02 in the laboratory with COMO to analyse the desired materials for radioactive contamination.
  • Use the GSP02 with our GAMMO TOOLs and go into the field on-site and take measurements In situ. (for example, mushrooms in the forest or venison at the market stall)
  • COMO is needed in a different location and no PC and/or power supply is available? In connection with GAMMO TOOLs, you are also flexible and non-mobile.

Detectors

  • 2" x 2" Na(Tl) (2 pieces) - resolution 8 % (137Cs 662 keV)
  • PIPS 1700 mm²
  • Coaxial germanium detector (HP Ge) with electric cooling - No liquid nitrogen required
  • Resolution 2.0 keV FWHM at 1.33 MeV - Relative efficiency 33% at 1.33 MeV
