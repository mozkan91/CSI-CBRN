CBRN Protective Gloves

CBRN Protective Gloves – Symmetrical Model

The CBRN Protective Gloves – Symmetrical Model are designed to provide a high level of protection against Chemical, Biological, Radiological, and Nuclear (CBRN) threats. Their symmetrical construction—featuring the same design for both the right and left hand—ensures ease of use and simplifies logistics.

Manufactured in accordance with the NATO AEP-38 standard, these gloves offer excellent resistance to hazardous chemicals and deliver reliable protection even during extended operations. The outer layer is made of butyl rubber, while the cotton inner liner enhances both comfort and wearability.

  • Spherical active carbon technology
  • Long-lasting and reliable protection
  • High mobility and superior user comfort
  • Manufacturing in compliance with international standards
  • Model options suitable for different mission requirements

Product Features

  • Model: Symmetrical (same design for right and left hand)
  • Standard: Manufactured in compliance with NATO AEP-38
  • Material: Butyl rubber
  • Chemical Resistance: High resistance to hazardous chemicals
  • Protection Duration: Effective protection for more than 24 hours
  • Inner Liner: Cotton inner liner for easier and more comfortable use
  • Available Sizes: S – M – L

Areas of Use

  • Military units and defense industry applications
  • Special operations against CBRN threats
  • Emergency response and civil defense teams
  • Industrial environments with chemical risk exposure
  • Disaster response and crisis management scenarios
