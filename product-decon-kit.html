Responder Decon Kit

Responder Decontamination Kit

The RDK (Responder Decon Kit) is a highly effective solution for mass casualty incidents, providing a quick and efficient way for both first responders and affected individuals to conduct immediate decontamination. It is a compact, waterless solution designed for immediate and self-performed decontamination in emergency scenarios. It’s ideal for forward deployment and scalable to different incident sizes. The kit includes three FiberTect wipes, each with color-coded labels and a specific purpose. The decontamination procedure is broken down into three easy steps, with an instruction card provided for detailed guidance.

  • Easy to carry and transport
  • User-friendly with clear instructions
  • Allows for immediate decontamination
  • Suitable for small to large-scale incidents
  • Speeds up the transition to further medical care
  • Enables victims to self-decontaminate, saving critical time
  • Reduces the burden on first responders by allowing individuals to + start the decon process themselves

User Group / Scope of Use

  • Armed Forces
  • CBRN Defense Units
  • Law Enforcement and Special Operations Forces
  • Authorized Emergency Response and Tactical Medical Personnel

Contents

    3 x FiberTect Polyester Wipes
  • 12 x 12 in. FiberTect Wipe (x1)
  • 6 x 12 in. FiberTect Wipe (x2)
