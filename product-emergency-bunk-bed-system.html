Emergency Bunk Bed System

Safe and Rapid Shelter Solution in Times of Crisis


Bunk Bed and Mattress Set

In situations such as war, mass migration, natural disasters, and humanitarian crises, the most fundamental need is a safe resting and sheltering area. Our Emergency Bunk Bed System provides a reliable solution for mass accommodation operations by ensuring rapid deployment in crisis zones, maximum capacity, and long-term durability.
Our Emergency Bunk Bed System has been specifically developed for mass sheltering operations conducted by relevant ministries, government authorities, municipalities, and national and international aid organizations.


Why Our Emergency Bunk Bed System?
  • Rapid deployment - Saves critical time in the field
  • High durability - Suitable for intensive use
  • Logistical advantage - More accommodation in minimal space
  • Long Service Life - Provides sustainable shelter infrastructure

Technical Specs

  • Closed Dimensions: 96 x 20 x 14 cm
  • Weight: 10 kg
  • Weight Capacity: > 140 kg
