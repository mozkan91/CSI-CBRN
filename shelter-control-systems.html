Automated Shelter Control Systems

Bringing intelligence and security to underground shelter and bunker systems.

Fully automated shelter control systems improve preparedness by integrating CBRNe, air quality and all shelter equipment as well as building management and maintenance into one single system.

  • Real-time data from all connected sensors
  • Shelter automations based on detection data and shelter atmosphere changes
  • Hardware independent solutions – Choose the most suitable technologies for your specific needs
  • All shelter systems controlled from one interface
  • Real-time system status – Filtration health, blast valves etc.
  • Can take over building management system features in emergency mode

Full monitoring and control system for CBRNe and shelter equipment

We have designed a solution that provides a whole new level of security and safety for your shelters. It combines and integrates all key aspects of the shelter into one interface and simplifies the control and functions of the shelter. ObSAS Shelter Control Systems provides unprecedented capabilities, ease of use and efficiency.

Platform and Hardware Independent Solution

Shelter Control Systems are designed to be open towards the technologies you wish to implement. You can choose from a wide variety of detectors, shelter equipment and 3rd party systems. We will then integrate them to work seamlessly in one interface with real-time measurements and status information at all times.

ObSAS Automated Shelter Control Systems is the core platform for our integrations. It is a robust but highly customisable CBRNe command and control system for visualising critical data and showing status of all the connected systems.

Starting point is the shelter blueprint where user can see the status of each shelter area, doors, valves and any measurement devices at a quick glance. If the devices integrated to the system detect anything unusual ObSAS will display a visual, audio and text notification of the issue.

Other panels can contain anything the user requires the system to show. Commonly it’s the status of the CBRN filtration systems O2 and CO2 systems, doors, cameras and outside weather measurements. However if the system also integrates to a building management system it’s features can be shown in the ObSAS interface as a part of the entire system.

All notable incidents are logged for later inspection and can be exported to be sent for proper authorities.

Works with all major shelter equipment providers

We can integrate shelter features of every major global shelter manufacturer’s system to our ObSAS Shelter Control Systems. You can monitor and control them from the ObSAS user interface.
  • Doors
  • Valves
  • Oxygen and carbon dioxide removal systems
  • CBRN filtration
  • Pressure monitoring and control systems
  • Fire safety
  • Surveillance

ObSAS Maintenance

ObSAS Maintenance integrates to ObSAS Shelter Control Systems. It's purpose is to make sure all devices integrated to your shelter are properly maintained according to manufacturers recommendations. It keeps track on spare parts and maintenance procedures.

ObSAS Maintenance guides the personnel and provides its users with the necessary manuals. Expensive and delicate instruments are of no use if they are not well maintained. ObSAS Maintenance organises maintenance tasks and makes them easy to track.
